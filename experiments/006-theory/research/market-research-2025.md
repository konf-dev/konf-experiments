## Business Models That Work

### LangChain/LangSmith

- Open-core: free framework (130M+ downloads), paid LangSmith platform
- ARR: $12-16M (June 2025)
- Monthly trace volume 12x'd YoY
- Expanding into AIOps

### Proven Patterns

| Model | Examples | Mechanics |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| Open-core | LangChain, n8n, Supabase | Free OSS + paid observability/hosting/enterprise |
| Usage-based | LangSmith, Supabase | Per-API-call, per-trace, per-row |
| Enterprise | Azure/AWS/GCP | Compliance, SLAs, support |
| Managed hosting | Supabase Cloud, n8n Cloud | Self-hosted free, hosted paid |

### Key Conversion Metrics

- AI buyers convert at 47% (vs SaaS 25%)
- PLG share: 27% (vs 7% traditional software)
- Open-core conversion: 1-3% community → paid
